Ro Sham Bo!

Ro Sham Bo also called rock paper scissors is a simple game of chance. Rock beats Scissor. Scissor beats Paper. Paper beats Rock. Take you chance against the computer. Click the Rock, Paper, or Scissors button to make your selection. You can always change your selection and click a different choice. When you clicked the button you wanted, click the Ro Sham Bo! button and play the game of chance!
